Balloon Payload Program 

Welcome to the Balloon Payload Program! (BPP for short) We fly high altitude balloons with payloads attached to collect scientific data or test out engineering concepts. Our balloons routinely travel to altitudes greater than 100,000 feet. We conduct an average of three to four launches a semester to test these payloads. We have also flown payloads for the HAPL lab as well as a variety of outreach projects with different schools and student groups.
